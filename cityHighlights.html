City Highlights

This page summarizes a few places and experiences that help define Owasso for residents and visitors.

For more city information, visit the official City of Owasso website.

Popular Owasso highlights
Place Type Best For What Visitors Notice
Redbud Park Park and event venue Concerts, community activities, family outings A large lawn, open-air pavilion, and a relaxed gathering atmosphere
Historic mural district Landmark and photo stop Learning local history and taking pictures Artwork that highlights earlier businesses and transportation
Downtown Owasso Business and dining area Shopping, restaurants, and evening strolls Walkable streets, storefronts, and a growing modern look
Community events Seasonal experiences Meeting neighbors and supporting local causes Strong volunteer spirit and family-friendly activities

Suggested One-Day Visit

  1. Start the morning with a walk through downtown Owasso.
  2. Stop for lunch and local shopping near the city center.
  3. Spend the afternoon at Redbud Park or another green space.
  4. Finish the day at a seasonal event or community gathering.
